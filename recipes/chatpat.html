CHATPATE

INGRIDENTS OF CHATPATE

  • Puff Rice
  • Onion
  • Potato
  • Chilly
  • Tomato
  • Waiwai Noodle
  • Coriander
  • Chat masala
  • Chana

HOW TO MAKE CHATPATE

  1. On a large bowl add all of the above mentioned ingredients
  2. Mix well
  3. Now your chatpate is ready
  4. Enjoy!!!!!!!!!!!
